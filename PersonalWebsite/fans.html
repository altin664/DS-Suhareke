KB Golden Eagle Ylli & Xhebrailat

KB. Golden Eagle Ylli, established in 1975, is one of Kosovo's most storied basketball clubs. Based in Theranda, the team has cultivated a rich history, marked by passionate support from their dedicated fan base known as "Xhebrailat." This fervent group plays a pivotal role in creating an electrifying atmosphere during home games at the 13 Qërshori sports hall, which accommodates approximately 1,300 spectators. GOLDENEAGLE-KS.COM The synergy between KB Ylli and Xhebrailat is palpable, with fans often orchestrating vibrant displays of support that include coordinated chants, banners, and a sea of team colors. This unwavering enthusiasm not only boosts player morale but also establishes a formidable home-court advantage, making the 13 Qërshori sports hall a challenging venue for visiting teams. In 2008, financial challenges led to the club's temporary withdrawal from the Kosovo Superleague. However, a resurgence occurred in 2014 when Golden Eagle became the main sponsor, facilitating KB Ylli's return to the league. This partnership heralded a new era of success, culminating in consecutive league titles during the 2020–21 and 2021–22 seasons. GOLDENEAGLE-KS.COM The commitment of Xhebrailat has been instrumental in this renaissance. Their relentless support, both home and away, underscores a deep-rooted community spirit and a shared passion for basketball. This bond between the team and its supporters exemplifies the profound impact of fan engagement in sports, where collective energy and dedication can propel a team to remarkable achievements.
